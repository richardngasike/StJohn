# Public Images Folder

Place your images in this folder. The following are expected:

## Required Images
- `logo.png` — College logo (recommended: 200x200px, PNG with transparent background)
- `logo-white.png` — White version of the logo for dark backgrounds

## Hero / Slideshow
- `hero-1.jpg` — First hero slide background (campus wide shot, recommended 1920x1080)
- `hero-2.jpg` — Second slide (graduation ceremony)
- `hero-3.jpg` — Third slide (students in class or lab)
- `hero-4.jpg` — Fourth slide (campus facilities)

## About Section
- `campus.jpg` — Campus photo for About section (800x600)
- `campus-aerial.jpg` — Aerial view of campus
- `library.jpg` — Library interior
- `lab.jpg` — Computer lab or science lab
- `classroom.jpg` — Classroom with students

## Graduation / Events
- `graduation-1.jpg` — Graduation ceremony photo
- `graduation-2.jpg` — Students in graduation gowns
- `convocation.jpg` — Convocation hall

## News / Blog Thumbnails
- `news-admissions.jpg` — Admissions news thumbnail
- `news-events.jpg` — Events news thumbnail
- `news-achievement.jpg` — Achievement news thumbnail
- `news-partnership.jpg` — Partnership news thumbnail

## Staff / Leadership
- `principal.jpg` — Principal photo
- `deputy-principal.jpg` — Deputy Principal photo
- `registrar.jpg` — Registrar photo
- `dean-students.jpg` — Dean of Students photo

## Pattern / Decorative
- `pattern.svg` — Decorative SVG pattern for backgrounds

## Recommended Image Sources
- Unsplash (free): https://unsplash.com/s/photos/university-kenya
- Pexels (free): https://www.pexels.com/search/african%20university/
- Your own college photography (best!)

## Format Tips
- Use WebP format for best performance
- Optimize images before uploading (use https://squoosh.app)
- Keep hero images under 500KB for fast loading
